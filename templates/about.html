About Simplify

Simplify is a powerful tool designed to help users extract and simplify complex legal and technical documents.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make legal and technical documents easier to understand by breaking them down into plain, simplified text. Whether you're a lawyer, a student, or anyone who needs to simplify complex documents, Simplify is here to assist.

How It Works

Simplify uses multiple datasets pertaining to Tos agreements, legal documents, and language simplification in order to fine-tune a LLM for our desired task. Simplify parses documents by splitting them into overlapping chunks and performs inference on each chunk. It then deploys NLP processes in order to clean and format the text.
